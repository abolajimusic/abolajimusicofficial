Big Announcement: New Album Release

Posted on Febuary 3 2024 by Abolaji

Abolaji Set to Take the Music Industry by Storm!* Lagos, Nigeria - Rising Afrobeats and R&B sensation, Abolaji, is gearing up to release his highly anticipated debut single, "Heartbeat." The upcoming track promises to be a game-changer in the music industry, blending traditional Yoruba sounds with modern production techniques. Exclusive Interview We caught up with Abolaji to discuss his upcoming project, inspirations, and future plans. "I'm thrilled to share my music with the world," Abolaji exclaimed. "Vickie Bae is a reflection of my soul, a song for my girl friend and I'm confident it will resonate with listeners globally." When asked about his influences, Abolaji cited legendary artists like Fela Kuti and King Sunny Adé, as well as contemporary icons like Wizkid and Davido. Debut Single: "Vicky Bae" Vicky Bae is slated for release in early 2024. The track features Abolaji's soulful vocals, infectious melodies, and a fusion of Afrobeats and R&B. *Upcoming EP: "Opsy The EP” Abolaji is also working on his debut EP, "Opsy The EP”,scheduled for release early August 2025.The project promises to showcase his versatility and talent. Collaborations and Live Performances Abolaji is set to collaborate with top producers and artists, including DJ Tunez and Mayorkun. Fans can expect electrifying live performances at upcoming festivals and concerts. Social Media Buzz Abolaji's social media platforms are abuzz with excitement.
